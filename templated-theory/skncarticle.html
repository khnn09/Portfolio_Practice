Student in Economics

My passion in Economics is rooted in its unique feature as both science and humanities. It requires mathematical thinking and involves solving social problems, for which math cannot provide a single numerical answer. That is why I believe that economics serves as a very powerful tool in seeing and dealing with social problems. It necessitates and presents a deep understanding of human nature. I hope to utilize my understanding of economics, which provides a precise and logical perspective on society, to improve the world.

Write: From Knowledge & With Purpose

I sought to combine my fascination in Economics and writing. I joinned the Editorial Board of Yonsei Journal of Economics and Business in Spring of 2019. Since then I have been involved in publication of 83rd, 84th, and 85th edition of the journal.
My articles include: "Truth Stands Alone - Public Trial System of Korea", "Magic of Randomization - How RCT Works", "The Train Goes Choo Choo - Privatization of Korean Railway"
